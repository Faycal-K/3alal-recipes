Tajine

Description

A tajine is a Moroccan dish that is healthy, easy to prepare, nutritious and cheap. You can add meat, chicken or fish if wanted.

Ingredients

These ingredients are for four persons:

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil;
  • 0,5 tablespoon of salt;
  • 500ml of water;
  • 500 grams of potatoes;
  • 250 grams of aubergine;
  • 150 grams of carrot;
  • 100 grams of olives;
  • 2 onions;
  • One loaf of Moroccan bread.

Steps

These are the steps to make this dish:

  1. Put 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a pan or tajine and put the tajine on medium fire
  2. Cut the onions and add them to the tajine
  3. While the onions are cooking, cut all the vegetables as you like
  4. If you see that the onions are cooked (but not burnt though), add the rest of the veggies to the tajine
  5. Add the rest of the olive oil to the tajine
  6. Add 250ml of water to the tajine
  7. Let the tajine cook and add the rest of the water when the water in the tajine is evaporated
  8. Whenever the veggies are cooked, you're done! Check the veggies every 10 minutes.
