Couscous

Description

Couscous is a Moroccan meal that is typically eaten on friday. It's eaten in one plate by multiple persons. You can add meat or chicken to it.

Ingredients

These ingredients are for four persons:

  • 450 grams of couscous;
  • 150 grams of carrot;
  • 150 grams of potatoes;
  • 150 grams of courgette;
  • Three tablespoons of olive oil;
  • 750ml of water;
  • A teacup of salt and spices.

Steps

These are the steps to make couscous

  1. Add spices and salt to the water
  2. Start cooking the water
  3. Cut the veggies and add them to the water so they can cook
  4. Steam the couscous on top of the water
  5. Whenever the couscous is cooked, put it in a bowl
  6. Whenever the veggies are cooked, take them out of the water and put hem aside
  7. Add half of the water (Marqa in Moroccan) to the couscous and mix the couscous
  8. Put the couscous on a plate, add the veggies on top and add the remaining Marqa to the couscous
